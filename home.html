Information

Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur and businessman.

Musk was born on June 28, 1971, in Pretoria, South Africa. He taught himself how to program, and when he was 12 he sold his first software : a game he created called Blastar.
Musk moved to Canada(1989) to attend Queen’s University, In 1992, Musk left Canada to study business and physics at the University of Pennsylvania. He graduated with an undergraduate degree in economics and stayed for a second bachelor’s degree in physics. Musk headed to Stanford University in California to pursue a PhD in energy physics. But he dropped out of Stanford after just two days to become a part of it, launching his first company, Zip2 Corporation in 1995.
Elon musk has received criticism for lots of time,sometimes due to tweets or sometimes due to controversial statements.
